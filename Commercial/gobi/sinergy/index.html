Chantsaldulam

Born and raised in the beautiful wild nature of Mongolia, I have discovered my true passion through the lenses of camera. During my experimental years, I had travelled as a part of new generation documentary makers to discover the Nomadic Culture and Lifestyle of Mongolia. Unlike many photographers, I love putting my lenses from the different point of view to discover a raw hidden gems of my culture. I truly believe that everything can be extraordinary if you can only see it. These days I am exploring more into the Mongolian fashion photography field. It is very fascinating and challenging. Even though my experience is small, my passion to create a beautiful yet meaningful photo is very big.
